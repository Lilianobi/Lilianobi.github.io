Welcome to Marley & Blue Coffee Shop – Bad Coffee is Over

At Marley & Blue, we believe coffee should be nothing short of exceptional. Located in the heart of Port-harcourt, our café is a haven for coffee lovers seeking the perfect brew in a warm and inviting space. Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso, savoring a handcrafted latte, or relaxing with a smooth cold brew, we ensure every cup is brewed to perfection—because life’s too short for bad coffee.

Our Coffee, Sourced with Passion

We proudly source our beans from the world’s finest coffee-growing regions—Ethiopia, Colombia, and Brazil—working with ethical and sustainable farms that share our passion for quality. Each batch is carefully roasted to highlight the unique flavors, delivering bold, rich, and smooth coffee in every sip.
More Than Coffee, It’s an Experience
At Marley & Blue, we don’t just serve coffee—we create moments. From our friendly baristas to our cozy atmosphere and delicious pastries, we invite you to sit back, unwind, and enjoy coffee the way it was meant to be.
Come in, take a seat, and raise your cup to Bad Coffee is Over!

ABOUT US

We are a community coffee shop located in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. Our cafe is a space to share ideas, reconnect with each other, learn, and champion the best in humanity.

Founded by Ese Emerhi, Marley & Blue is a small family-run business. It has always been a dream of hers to own a coffee shop and to bring home that sense of community that coffee shops are known for globally. We take pride in connecting with our customers personally, and our promise and motto are "Bad coffee is over!"

We provide safe and condusive environment with a warm interior decor.

Variety is the spice of life. We offer custom made herbal mocktails with our coffee keeping you active.

We are a phone call away. Orders and payment can be placed on mobile.

EXPRESSO

Expresso has an Italy origin with strong, bold,and concentrated with a rich crema on top.
It is made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee under high pressure using an espresso machine.

AMERICANO

₦2,000

Americano is believed to have been popularized by American soldiers in Italy during WWII. It is dmooth and slightly diluted, but retains espresso’s rich taste.
Espresso shot topped with hot water, giving it a milder strength than espresso but stronger than black coffee.

CAPPUCCINO

₦2,700

Cappuccino originates from Italy, a balanced mix of strong espresso, creamy milk, and a light, airy froth. Cappuccino is made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, often topped with cocoa or cinnamon.

LATTE

₦1,500

Latte has its origin from Italy. Flavor is creamy and smooth with a mild coffee taste due to more milk.
Latte is made with one shot of espresso and a larger amount of steamed milk, topped with light milk foam.

BLACK COFFEE

₦1,000

Black coffee is Global, as it is one of the simplest ways to drink coffee. Flavor contains pure coffee taste, often bitter.
Black coffee is made by brewing coffee with hot water using methods like drip, pour-over, or French press, without milk or sugar.

COLD BREW

₦3,000

Cold brew is traced back to Japan and later popularized in the U.S. It is known to have Smooth, slightly sweet, and less acidic than hot coffee.
Cold brew is made by immersing coarse coffee grounds steeped in cold water for 12-24 hours, then strained and served chilled over ice.
