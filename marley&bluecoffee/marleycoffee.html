ABOUT US

We are a community coffee shop located in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. Our cafe is a space to share ideas, reconnect with each other, learn, and champion the best in humanity. Founded by Ese Emerhi, Marley & Blue is a small family-run business. It has always been a dream of hers to own a coffee shop and to bring home that sense of community that coffee shops are known for globally. We take pride in connecting with our customers personally, and our promise and motto are "Bad coffee is over!"
